# Announcing GNU Radio Conference 2026 (GRCon26) - Raleigh, NC

We’re excited to announce that **GNU Radio Conference 2026 (GRCon26)** will be held **in person** from **September 21 – 24, 2026**, in **Raleigh, North Carolina!**  


This year’s conference is generously hosted by [**AERPAW**](https://aerpaw.org/about/) at **North Carolina State University** and will take place in the beautiful [**Talley Student Union**](https://studentcenters.ncsu.edu/location/talley/).

---

## About GRCon26

**GRCon** celebrates and showcases the incredible progress of GNU Radio and its use across a wide range of applications and industries.  
The 2026 conference will run **Monday through Thursday**, featuring four full days of **high-quality technical content**, **hands-on workshops**, and **valuable networking opportunities**.

Attendees include professionals and enthusiasts from **industry, academia, government, and the open-source community** - all coming together to share ideas, learn, and collaborate.  
GRCon is where **theory meets practice**: a place to highlight real-world designs, implementations, and discoveries built on the open source SDR ecosystem.
